About Rubbie Kelvin

Rubbie Kelvin is a young and inspiring, Nigerian self-taught full stack developer, recognized for his groundbreaking innovations and ouside-the-box problem- solving strategies. His extensive expertise in technological concepts has enabled him to contribute meaningfully to society, by consistently creating useful products and offering guidance to aspiring tech professionals.

Rubbie's passion for technology was ignited in his teenage years, when a team of robotics engineers visited his high school to prompt and encourage the students to explore careers in tech. Prior to this event, he was a naturally-gifted artist who enjoyed drawing and dreamed of owning his own comic bookstore. However, after his encounter with the robotics engineers, his focus shifted more toward robotics. He immersed himself in science books and was briefly mentored by his math teacher. In a short while following this, he built his first robot car, made with paper. Not long after, he left robotics in pursuit of skills in software development.From that point forward, he has prospered within this discipline, forging a distinguished professional path. Overtime, he has pioneered and led teams, earning the respect of both beginners and experts owing to his excellent work ethic. He has also made significant contributions to numerous open-source projects and has partnered with other engineers to create high-impact solutions.

The journey and success story of this young, astute software engineer, inspires many. Those who know him personally often attest to his warmth and unwavering willingness to help others in need, in any way that he is able to.
